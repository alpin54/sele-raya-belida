Sele Raya Agri

Growing Forests, Empowering Communities

A 10,000-hectare teak legacy built on social forest management and FSC standards. We protect the planet while creating tangible prosperity for the people of Southeast Sulawesi.

Visit Website

Executive Overview

PT Sele Raya Agri operates under the official industrial plantation forest permit (IUPHHK-HTI) granted by the Minister of Forestry (SK. 736/Menhut-II/2014). Spanning 18,980 hectares across Muna and West Muna Regency, our project is located in a region world-renowned for its teak-growing heritage.

Our site offers the "Golden Standard" for teak cultivation:

Fertile Soil

Nutrient-rich land optimized for timber density.

Perfect Climate

Consistent rainfall and an average temperature of 28°C (84.2°F).

Social Harmony

A long-standing history of teak cultivation that is deeply respected and supported by the local community.

Vision

To be the global pioneer in sustainable forestry by establishing the largest premium teak plantation in the world, set in the heart of Muna, Southeast Sulawesi, Indonesia.

Mission

To develop a world-class social forest management program that integrates local communities, restores the environment, and delivers premium timber through sustainable innovation.

Strategic Objectives

We are building a business model where profitability and planetary health go hand-in hand. Our primary goals include

Sustainable Growth

Achieving long-term profitability through scale and efficiency.

Premium Certification

Securing FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) certification to meet international standards.

Large-Scale Reforestation

Establishing a 10,000 hectare teak plantation alongside a regenerative agroforestry system.

Our Operations Innovation & Efficiency

We have redefined traditional teak farming by combining nature’s strengths with modern silviculture techniques.

Innovation & Efficiency

We have redefined traditional teak farming by combining nature's strengths with modern silviculture techniques. While traditional teak takes over 30 years to mature, our accelerated growth methodology using high-quality vegetative seedlings and intensive cultivation reduces the harvest cycle to just 10 years.

In House Nursery

Every tree begins its life in our own specialized nursery, ensuring 100% quality control from seed to timber.

Beyond Timber

We maximize land value by promoting Non-Timber Forest Products (NTFP), including organic honey and natural fabric dyes, creating a diverse and resilient ecosystem.

Our Commitment Community & Environment
At PT Sele Raya Agri, we believe a forest is only as strong as the community that guards it. We bridge the gap between environmental conservation and economic prosperity through partnership with nature and humanity.

Regenerative Agroforestry

We don't just plant trees; we build food systems. Through our social forestry program, we empower local farmers to utilize the land between teak rows to grow:

  • Corn
  • Soybeans
  • Peanuts

By teaching best practices in sustainable farming, we increase local food security and provide immediate income for families while the teak matures.

Global Responsibility

As we pursue the FSC International Certification, we guarantee our global partners that our products are ethically sourced, environmentally sound, and socially beneficial. We are not just selling wood; we are providing a sustainable solution for a high-demand global market.

Why Choose PT Sele Raya Agri?

Scale

Access to one of the largest teak reserves in the region.

Speed

A 10-year rotation period that ensures a faster return on investment and supply.

Sustainability

Fully integrated social and regenerative programs that meet global ESG standards.
