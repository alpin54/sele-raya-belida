Sele Raya Sejati

A Three-Decade Legacy of Energy Excellence

A 30 year strategic collaboration between PT Sele Raya Sejati, Medco Energi, and Kufpec to unlock 50 MMBO and 450 BCF of energy potential in South Sumatra.

OVERVIEW

Strategic Growth Opportunity Amanah Block

The Amanah Block represents a significant expansion of PT Sele Raya Sejati’s portfolio in the energy-rich region of South Sumatra. Through a strategic collaboration with PT Medco Energi Amanah and Kufpec Indonesia (Amanah) B.V., this project is positioned to be a key driver of regional energy production.

The partnership combines technical expertise and local operational excellence to unlock the full potential of this high-prospect onshore asset.
