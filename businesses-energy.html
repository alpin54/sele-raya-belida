Sele Raya Energy

Fueling the Future Strategic Gas Production in the East Java Basin.

With a proven track record in the Blora Block and the successful development of the Rembang Field, PT Sele Raya Energi delivers sustainable energy solutions through expertise and strategic partnerships.

Project History & Milestones

Our journey with the Blora Block began with a rigorous technical foundation and a commitment to long-term energy production.

Joint Study Phase

Conducted an intensive joint study with Migas from October 2008 to January 2009

Tender Award

Successfully granted the "Direct Offer Tender" on July 30, 2009

PSC Signing

The Production Sharing Contract (PSC) was officially signed on November 30, 2009, with BPMIGAS

Asset Profile The Blora Block

The Blora Block is a high-potential asset located in the Central-East Java region. Situated within the renowned East Java Basin, this onshore gas field offers a proven petroleum system and a strategic location adjacent to existing, productive oil and gas fields.

Proven Discovery

The block features the Rembang Field, a confirmed gas discovery that underscores the area's high commercial value.

Strategic Operations

The block is currently operated by TIS Petroleum E&P Blora Pte. Ltd., ensuring efficient production workflows.
