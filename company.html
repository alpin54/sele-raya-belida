Who We Are

  • Block Name

    Belida Block (Onshore)

  • Total Area

    678.11km²

Our journey began in 2004 when we were officially appointed as the operator of the Belida Block in South Sumatera. Since our first oil discovery in August 2008, we have consistently expanded our footprint, currently operating two high-potential fields: Cantik Field and Sungai Anggur Field.

PT Sele Raya Belida (SRB) is a prominent subsidiary of the Sele Raya Group, one of Indonesia’s most established energy holding companies. With nearly 20 years of active contribution to the national energy sector, we have built a reputation for operational excellence and strategic growth.

  • Contract Type

    Production Sharing Contract (PSC)

  • Key Producing Fields

    Cantik Field & Sungai Anggur Field

Mission & Vision

Strategic Impact & Sustainability

As a proud member of the Sele Raya Group, we are dedicated to unlocking Indonesia’s energy potential through innovative and responsible production.

We go beyond traditional extraction; our ultimate goal is to support Indonesia’s national production targets while creating long-term value for our stakeholders. We are actively promoting the transition toward clean, sustainably-sourced energy, ensuring that our operations today contribute to a greener and more stable tomorrow.
