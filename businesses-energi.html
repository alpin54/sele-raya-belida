PT Sele Energi

Premium Meranti, Responsibly Sourced.

Over 2.9 Million m3 of high-quality timber potential, managed under the highest Sustainable Forest Management (PHPL) standards.

A Legacy of Visionary Leadership
Sele Energi is a premier, privately owned energy group headquartered in Jakarta. Since our founding in 1972, we have transitioned from a specialized technical pioneer into a formidable regional player within the oil and gas industry.

Our Identity

Built on over 40 years of operational excellence, we combine deep Indonesian roots with world-class management values.

The 6 Cs Culture

Our group is driven by a commitment to being Caring, Concentrated, Competitive, Creative, Communicative, and Committed.

Strategic Advantage

We operate with a "National Operations, International Vision" mindset. Our streamlined organizational structure ensures agility, allowing us to move fast and decisively to capitalize on emerging energy opportunities.

Strategic Assets & Performance

We specialize in upstream exploration and production (E&P), delivering high-performance results through a robust portfolio of concessions and an unrivaled safety record.

Strategic Portfolio

Merangin Dua Block (2,847 sq km) is a producing high-yield asset, Belida Block (3,400 sq km) is development-ready with an approved POD, and Blora Block (~5,000 sq km) offers strong exploration potential within a proven petroleum system.

The Gold Standard in Safety

We take pride in an impeccable record of zero fatalities over 40 years. This achievement reflects our mastery over technically complex projects and our "Safety First" mission.

Primed for Expansion

We are aggressively pursuing growth through the acquisition of offshore and frontier assets, supported by world-class international partnerships and access to global capital.

Corporate Social Responsibility
We believe that our success should benefit the communities surrounding us. PT Amindo Wana Persada is deeply committed to improving the quality of life in local villages such as Long Lamcin, Batu Rajang, and Long Suluy.

Rigorous Governance

Integrity is our foundation. To ensure the highest standards of accountability, we undergo four external audits annually by government authorities and global firms, including PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) and Ernst & Young (EY).

Empowering Human Capital

Providing training, development programs, and support to enhance the skills and capabilities of our employees and the local community.

Driving Community Prosperity

Providing diesel fuel, lighting, and regular road repairs for surrounding villages.
