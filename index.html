Who We Are

A Legacy of Energy Excellence

As a core subsidiary of the Sele Raya Group, we bring nearly 20 years of expertise to Indonesia’s energy landscape. Since signing our first Production Sharing Contract in 2004, we have evolved from a strategic explorer into a high performing operator in the South Sumatra Basin. Our journey is defined by a "World-Class" vision and a mission to drive national economic welfare through responsible asset optimization.
